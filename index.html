What is it

A pub crawl, sometimes known as a barathon, is a tour of different pubs and clubs in the area of your choice.

Who is it for

For all ages from 18 +. All you need is your ID to join the party!

How does it work

It all starts at the meeting point where we will provide you with a wristband that gives you access to all the clubs we will party to.

Where do we meet

Depending on your choice, the meeting point will be written on the ticket. Contact us if you have any questions.
